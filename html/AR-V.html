Summary[edit]

DescriptionBandera de la Provincia de Tierra del Fuego.svg
English: Flag of Tierra del Fuego Province, Argentina. The flag was nicknamed the Albatross, wherein the orange portion reflected the geography of Tierra del Fuego, and the blue symbolizes the sky and sea surrounding the province, while the Southern Cross reflects the night sky and the albatross itself is a local bird that represents freedom through flight.[1]
Date 1999 (Promulgated by Provincial Decree Nº 1794)
